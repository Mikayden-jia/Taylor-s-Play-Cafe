Welcome to Taylor’s Play Cafe

Welcome to Taylor’s Play Cafe – where playtime for kids meets me-time for you!

We’ve created a warm, welcoming space where both children and adults can thrive. Whether you're a busy parent, a remote worker, or someone needing a break, our cafe offers the perfect balance of fun and relaxation.

Let your little ones explore, learn, and play in our thoughtfully designed play area while you enjoy a coffee, get some work done, or simply breathe. It's your time, too.

Open daily from 9:00 AM to 9:00 PM – swing by for a morning adventure or an evening wind-down. We’re always ready to welcome you!

About Us

Taylor’s Play Cafe was built with families in mind. We believe in the power of imaginative play and quality downtime for parents. Our spaces are designed to be fun, safe, and engaging for kids, while adults enjoy a cozy, modern environment just steps away.

We’re more than a play area – we’re a little community, where smiles, creativity, and caffeine come together beautifully.

Our Menu

Grab a bite while the little ones burn energy. We proudly serve a range of hot and cold beverages including coffee and non-coffee options. Our food menu features hearty rice meals, delicious sandwiches, sweet treats, and savory bites to satisfy every craving.

Everything is prepared fresh and made to keep you energized and smiling through every visit.
